General Principles and Rules

1.	The Students' Disciplinary Code is aimed at: 

    1.1	upholding the name and reputation of the University;

    1.2	maintaining order, discipline, safety and security at the University;

    1.3	ensuring the integrity of the academic processes of the University; and

    1.4	assuring the quality of the assessment processes at the University.
                
2.	Any conduct of a student which contravenes the Disciplinary Code and/or negatively impacts on these goals may be regarded as misconduct and
    subject to disciplinary measures.

3.	The general supervision and control of student discipline at the University vests with the
    Registrar and are administered in terms of the Students' Disciplinary Code of the University.
